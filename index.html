Travel Experience to Fire Island

My travel to Fire Island was facilitated by my former secondary highschool when I was in form four in Mombasa county. Although I was exposed to very many new things and sights during my four weeks stay,the most memorable experience was visiting together with my friends and our teachers. The uniqueness of the Island is grounded on its capacity to host major wildlife attractions and ease of accessibility from the main land. It is located 4km away from the mainland of Mombasa county, hence tourist can easily land at Moi International Airport and have the opportunity to visit the island by sea which offers the use of Ferries or by Air which offers the use of small planes capable of landing on small Airstrips or Helicopters. Interms of wildlife attrctions, there are boat safaris around the Island giving you different beautiful sceneries, snorkeling, scuba diving observing the marine life on the shores example: Dolphins, sea turtles, crabs etc. Our tour guide also informed us that there are acouple of land wild life as well,example: Elephants, girrafes and zebra. We were also shown the exquisite camping sites aswell. The nightlife was filled with lots of awesome activities such as, stargazing and astronomy, cooking, singing and playing musical instruments, playing illuminated games. Todate, the visit at the Island still remains my most memorable experience due the many wildlife attractions and hospitable people. It was the most fulfilling experience of my life
